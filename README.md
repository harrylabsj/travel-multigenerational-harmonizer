# Multi-Generational Travel Harmonizer
Creates travel experiences that work for multiple generations traveling together — from grandparents to toddlers.
## What it does
- Conducts a **need assessment framework** with interest surveys for each generation and mobility-friendly accommodation filters
- Implements an **activity layering system** with morning (gentle pace), afternoon (active), and evening (all-together) tiers
- Provides **pace management strategies** including rest-day scheduling, split-and-meet models, and opt-out-without-guilt policies
## Example scenarios
**1. 带爸妈和小孩一起去三亚**
> 👤 想暑假带爸妈（60多岁）和小孩（6岁）一起去三亚玩一周，怎么安排才能大家都满意？
> 🤖 输出三代同堂三亚行程——住宿（公寓式酒店两室一厅可做饭比两间房便宜）、活动分配（上午全家沙滩/森林公园、下午老人午休+年轻人带孩子去水世界）、交通（携程租车约200/天比打车方便）、餐饮（清淡海鲜餐厅+可自己做粥面），备好防暑药和防晒用品。

**2. 7-day family trip with grandparents and kids**
> 👤 Plan a 7-day trip for my family: grandparents (70s), us (40s), and kids (8 & 12). Budget $12K.
> 🤖 Day-by-day itinerary with activity tiers — morning (gentle pace for grandparents), afternoon (active for middle generation with kids), evening (all-together) — with mobility-friendly accommodations, rest-day scheduling, dining that satisfies all palates, and contingency plans.

**3. Italy with conflicting preferences**
> 👤 Grandparents want to relax, teenagers want adventure, we want culture. How do we not kill each other in Italy for 10 days?
> 🤖 Split-and-meet schedule model — morning independent blocks (grandparents: piazza + café, teens: bike tour, parents: museum), afternoon family activities (cooking class, villa pool), evening dinners together — with communication norms and 'opt-out without guilt' policy.
